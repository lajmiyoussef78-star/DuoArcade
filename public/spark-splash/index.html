Spark & Splash

⚙ SETTINGS

SPARK (fire): A / D move, W jump  •  SPLASH (water): ← / → move, ↑ jump  •  P pause  •  R restart
Gold buttons open gates forever. Teal pads must be held down — stand on them or push a crate onto them. Ride elevators, climb with crates, and help each other reach the glowing doors!
